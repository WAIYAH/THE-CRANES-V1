Our History

Founded in 2002, Kanunka FC emerged from humble beginnings in the village center of Kanunka. With a fierce determination to unite the community through football, the club has grown into a powerhouse.

Key milestones include winning the first league title in 2007, the iconic 2023 treble, and consistent performances that have made The Cranes a feared opponent in every competition.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To inspire greatness on and off the pitch, fostering talent and community spirit.

Vision

To be a globally recognized club, celebrated for excellence, passion, and innovation.
